III. My Background

Below you can find a few organizations I am actively involved in and the roots which have led me to where I am now.

Organization Experience Roots
Virginia Commonwealth University This is my second year at VCU and thus far, I have taken a variety of foundational science and business courses. Some of my favorites being Winning Presentations and Chemistry II. I decided on VCU due to their stellar health programs, cultural diversity, and close proximity to my family in Northern Virginia. My choice to attend VCU allowed me to pursue both of my passions.
FACT at VCU FACT stands for Filipino Americans Coming Together. I am currently a part of their council, serving as Cultural Liaison. Essentially, I educate our general body on the cultural history and traditions of the Philippines. I was drawn to FACT due to my desire to be part of a Filipino community throughout my university experience.I strived to learn more about the roots of my own culture and share in the experiences of other Filipinx youth.
The PUSO Foundation The PUSO Foundation is a nonprofit organization that conducts humanitarian philanthropy worldwide, particularly in the Philippines. I am currently a PUSO college ambassador. My involvement in PUSO began due to my hopes of contributing to the welfare of my mother country and providing my peers with the opportunity of partaking in international service efforts.
